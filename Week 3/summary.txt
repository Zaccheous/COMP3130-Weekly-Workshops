Over half of the respondents have used duolingo before. 
With that in mind, only half of the users who completed the survey were able to successfully navigate to their friends list and add another user. 
This suggests that the UI should be clearer and is confusing even for returning users to the application. 
Users also reported issues navigating the application when attempting to change between lessons or pause their progress mid-lesson. 
Users also reported issues with too many tabs and steps to complete simple tasks. 
While the app is confusing for most users, overall feedback suggested that the aesthetics of duolingo is good. 
Furthermore, very few people said they were average and excellent. 
Overall, there wasn’t any negative comments on the visual design of the app.
